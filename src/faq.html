Frequently Asked Questions

How does your SmartWatch take blood samples?

Our technology does not use a needle to get blood samples to return a reading on glucose levels. Instead sensors embeded inside the bottom of the watch-face detect glucose levels through the skin in your hand.

How does your technology work?

There exists minute amounts of blood in the capilaries of your skin. Our technology uses these minute amounts of blood to get readings on glucose levels through out the day.

Do I have to manually trigger the measurement of my glucose?

No! Our SmartWatch monitors glucose levels over 100 times a day, providing reliable and constant glucose monitoring.

Yes! In addition to its basic features, our device aims to include diabetic individuals by helping them monitor their blood-glucose levels without requiring any supplementary accessories.
